About Chhath Puja

Chhath is an ancient Hindu festival dedicated to Surya Dev (Sun God) and Chhathi Maiya. Devotees offer arghya to the setting and rising sun, observing fasting and holy bathing as acts of gratitude and devotion.

Schedule

  • Evening Arghya: Sunset — gather at terrace or water area.
  • Morning Arghya: Sunrise — assemble before displayed sunrise time.
