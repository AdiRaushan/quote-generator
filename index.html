About Chhath Puja

Chhath is an ancient Hindu festival dedicated to the Sun God (Surya) and Chhathi Maiya. Devotees observe rigorous fasts, offer prayers and perform arghya (offerings) to the rising and setting sun. The festival is observed mainly in Bihar, Jharkhand, eastern Uttar Pradesh and parts of Nepal, and among their diaspora. Key rituals include holy bathing, fasting, standing in water for arghya at sunset and sunrise, and offering prasad and traditional items like thekua.
